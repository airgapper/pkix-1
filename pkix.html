PKIX

PKIX, the PacketFrame Internet Exchange, is an experimental IXP operated by PacketFrame for network experimentation.
All networks are encouraged to join if they meet the following conditions.

  • Have a public ASN from an ICANN-recognized RIR.
  • Have at least a /24 IPv4 and/or a /48 IPv6 allocation.
  • Peer over both IPv4 and IPv6 whenever possible.
  • Peer with both route servers.
  • Maintain an accurate and up to date PeeringDB profile.

Connection

PKIX supports physical cross connects via 1GigE Copper as well as 1GigE and 10GigE 850nm multimode fiber. Facilities can be found below. We also offer virtual connections through ZeroTier.

Configuration is built automatically from PeeringDB. At a minimum, you must have your ASN, Network Name, and AS-SET up to date in PeeringDB.

Contact info@packetframe.com to set up your session.
